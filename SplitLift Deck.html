03 · How It Works

Three steps to a split, in under thirty seconds.

01 · INPUT

Sport, days, height, weight.

One screen, four fields. Optional details collapse out of the way. No account, no logging, no chat.

02 · GENERATE

Score 800+ exercises against your sport profile.

The engine balances volume across muscle groups, weighted toward the demands of your sport.

03 · ADJUST

Drag, drop, and watch coverage shift.

Every change re-scores the body map in real time. Lock the days you like; regenerate the rest.
