SplitLift uses the "Myology" 3D model from Z-Anatomy.

Source:    https://sketchfab.com/3d-models/myology-31b40fd809b14665b93773936d67c52c
Author:    Z-Anatomy (Gauthier Kervyn)
License:   Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 International (CC BY-SA 4.0)
           https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/

Modifications applied for SplitLift:
- Decimated to ~50% triangle count via gltf-transform
- Geometry compressed with Draco (KHR_draco_mesh_compression)
- Textures compressed to WebP

Original model is itself a derivative of "BodyParts3D" (Database Center for Life
Science, Japan), also distributed under CC BY-SA. The CC BY-SA license requires
that any redistribution credit the original authors and use the same license.
